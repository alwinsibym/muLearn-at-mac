What's μLearn

μLearn simply is an awesome space where students could hangout for the purpose of learning and skill development. mulearn is a student driven community where students decides everything, and is supported by expert mentors behind the scenes. It is an all kerala community initiated by Gtech (Group of Technmology comminities), for the purpose of bridging the gap between industry and students and make students job ready. Here we learn as a group from each other and helps each others learn.
