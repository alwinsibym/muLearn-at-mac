Working Ecosystem of µLearn.

The Academia and Technology Focus Group (ATFG) framework was created and maintained to assist students in becoming industry ready. µLearn, associated with this ATFG framework, is a synergic philosophy of education with a culture of mutual learning through micro peer groups. This ecosystem, harbouring a network of participants such as universities, online communities, nodal agencies and the industry, integrates its participants and their contributions into a holistic system, providing mutual benefits to all.

About

Welcome to µLearn, a vibrant and inclusive community at the forefront of redefining education. Rooted in a synergic philosophy, µLearn fosters a culture of mutual learning through dynamic micro peer groups. Our commitment extends beyond traditional educational boundaries, as we strive to assist individuals in breaking through echo chambers and liberating themselves from self-imposed constraints. Each week, µLearn hosts a myriad of enriching events, brimming with compelling stories, transformative learning experiences, and inspirational moments. These events serve as key gateways to meaningful interactions, providing a platform where diverse perspectives converge, fostering personal and collective growth.


Join engaging events, like Inspiration Radio, and be part of a community that empowers students, mentors, and facilitators. Anchored by the ATFG framework, µLearn integrates universities, online communities, and industry stakeholders for a dynamic educational experience. Shape the future with us!

What is a µLearn Chaptership?

At µLearn, the concept of learning comes with a motivation and that at µLearn is the Gamification of learning. At µLearn a Chaptership is a recognition, acknowledgement given to a Campus when they unlock the level 5 of Activities .This Acknowledgement unlocks many benefits for these campuses. Campuses with the Chaptership title gets access to exclusive perks from the parent, The µLearn Foundation.
