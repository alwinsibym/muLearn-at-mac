How to Join Us ??

muLearn is an all kerala level community, every mulearn member has his/her own profile and a dashboard. So the first step is to register for a muLearn account/profile and get your muId which your userId.

1.1 Goto app.muLearn and you'll see a registration form as below.

Fill the details there and continue. No ned to select any communities, as you are not part of any communities. Also there is no need to fill refferal muId either. Now continue to next step

1.2 In the next step you must choose a suitable option, in your case it should be I'm currently Studying

Now Continue to next step

1.3 This step is to collect your college details. Choose Mar. Augusthinose College, Ramapuram as your college and then select your department and year of passing out

Now Submit the registration form

Congratulations! You've successfully created your muLearn profile

muLearn works inside Discord, there we have our own server and a bunch of channels. So to become part of muLearn you must first Create an account in discord and join muLearn server in Discord.

2.1 Goto Play store and download Discord app If you are using other devices You may go here

2.2 After downloading Discord Create an account in it and login to the app.

2.3 Now Goto your muLearn profile and click on Connect Discord option there.

You'll be redirected to Discord Server of muLearn

2.4 Click on Accept Invite option and join muLearn Server

Congratulations! You've successfully joined muLearn Discord Server

After joining muLearn you must accept our rules and conditions. muLearn is super strict with rules. If we find out you violating any of our rules, you'll be kicked out of the community for sure!

3.1 In a couple of minutes after joining the server you'll get a pop-up like belo, only after accepting it can you remain in the community. You can also find these same rules in the rules-and-readme channel of our server

After that continue to next step

You now have a muLearn account and a Discord account. But currently they are disconnected. We need to connect them inorder to see your performance, points etc... in your muLearn profile. So let's do that.

4.1 Goto personal messages section in your Discord app. There you can see a bot named AARONCHETTAN. Open that chat. You should've already got a message ending with a connect muid option. Click on that connect muid. When you click on that you'll see an input box popped up.

Do this if you cannot find aaronchettan or his message??

If you cannot find aaronchettan in your personal chat nor his message do the following

Go to #self-introduction channel and sent #ge-self-intro and you'll instantly get a message from aaronchettan. If you are still not getting it, feel free to connect with our core team members.

4.2 Now goto your muLearn Dashboard and copy your muId from there. Every muId's ends with @mulearn

4.3 Now return to Discord and paste your muId in the popup you got when you clicked on connect muId option in the message from Aaronchettan

Then click submit and you'll get a confirmation message

Congratulations! You've successfully created your muLearn profile, Joined muLearn discord server and finally you've connected your muId with your Discord account!! It's time for you to start doing some tasks and get yourself familiarise with the server. For now lets do the first task self-introduction

Now it's time for you to introduce youself to the community, (Before that make sure your discord profile picture is updated to your own photo. Your face must be clear.)

After changing you profile picture,

goto #self-introduction channel and sent a message introducing yourself including your name, college, course and which year you are (eg 2nd year BCA), nad your interests. Do not forget to include #ge-self-intro hashtag.

example message:
#ge-self-intro
Hello,
I am Christo John, a second year BCA student from Mar. Augusthinose college.
Interested in web development, uiux and Artificial Intgelligence.

Yo're all set to embark on a journey of excellence. After this you may find tasks that suits you and start doing the. You can find tsaks in the lvl1-info, lvl2-info and lvl3-info channels. You should gain atleast 400 karma points from lvl2 tasks and 800 karma points from lvl3 tasks once you do get that much, then only you can be considered as a true muLearn member. More on that in the muLearn a Beginners guide section. Before that you must fill our Campus chapter Form.

muLearn has affiliations with numerous colleges all across kerala. Most of them are engineering colleges. Luckily here at mar. Augusthinose college, we have an affiliation for ourselves with muLearn. mulearn has established campus chapters in each and every of their affiliated colleges. A campus chapter is like a branch of muLearn at a college. Interested students can join muLearn via these campus chapters. Evey campus chapter has a core team which taskes care of their campus chapter. The head of this core team must be a faculty(probaily an asst.proffessor) of the college, and other members are chosen from interested students themself. They strive hard to attract more students to this community and increase ovberall strength and standing of their college in muLearn.

In our college, muLearn was first introduced and started its activities on 22 March 2023. As of today we have 115 muLearn members in our college's campus chapter. But only a handful of students <60 are actively participating. Therefore to track interested active students and to give proper guidance to them, we the core members of campus chapter @ MAC opened a google form. Anyone who joined in muLearn and connected their muId with their discord account can register in this form. You must be a student at Mar. Augusthinose College as well. Once you fill up this form you will be added to one of the 6 teams in our college. Each team is led by one of our core team members who are skilled and knowledgeable enough to give you directions and point out your mistakes. So if you are someone who is looking forward to be an active member in mulearn you must fill this form as soon as you connect your muId with your discord account. Within 24hrs of filling this form, you'll be assigned to a team and will egt your team lead who is incharge of you. They'll guide you in doing tasks and with everything related to muLearn. We are a community of like minded people who are always ready to do everything possible for the wellbeing of our community and that's what we are. The only thing that matters is whether you are interested enough...
